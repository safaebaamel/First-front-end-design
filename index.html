About the idea

Jodi Picoult once said: “You can always edit a bad page. You can’t edit a blank page.", the quantity of wisedom in this quote took me to a high new level, so the moment I read that sentence I decided to make something so I can shout out everything I hold in my chest. The idea may not seem that big, but inside each one of us a big dream waiting to catch the smallest light in it's way.
